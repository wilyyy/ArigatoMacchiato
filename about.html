Our Mission

“While many of us find ourselves relaxing inside comfort of our own homes, we miss the social aspect of casual discussion and chatter about our hobbies and the things we love. That’s why we here at Arigato Macchiato wanted to create a second home for our customers to relax and destress after a hard day at work. Enjoy your favourite manga in the comfort atmosphere we offer to you, warm and cozy drinks to complement your reading adventure, and, most importantly, the presence of likeminded individuals to broaden your horizons.”
